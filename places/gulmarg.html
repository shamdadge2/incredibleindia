GULMARG

Where snow meets serenity

About Gulmarg

Gulmarg is a famous hill station in Jammu & Kashmir, known for lush green meadows, breathtaking views, and world-class skiing facilities.

Best Time to Visit Gulmarg

  • December – March: Snow and skiing
  • April – June: Meadows and sightseeing

Places to Visit in Gulmarg

Gulmarg Gondola

One of the highest cable cars in the world.

Alpather Lake

Frozen lake surrounded by snow peaks.

Ski Slopes

Internationally famous skiing destination.

Important Precautions

  • Follow local advisories
  • Wear proper winter gear
  • Pre-book activities
