UDAIPUR

“The Venice of the East, adorned with lakes and palaces.”

About Udaipur

Udaipur is often called the most romantic city in India. Set around a series of artificial lakes and lavish royal palaces, it offers a picturesque backdrop of the Aravalli Hills. The city is famous for its Rajput-era palaces, courtyards, and gardens.

Lake Pichola forms the heart of the city, with the magnificent Lake Palace seeming to float on its waters.

Best Time to Visit Udaipur

  • September to March: Pleasant weather, ideal for sightseeing
  • August: Monsoon greenery adds to the beauty
  • April to June: Summer heat prevents outdoor activities

Top Places to Visit in Udaipur

City Palace

A massive palace complex offering splendid views of the lake.

Lake Pichola

The central lake famous for boat rides and sunset views.

Jag Mandir

An exquisite island palace in Lake Pichola.

Saheliyon Ki Bari

The 'Garden of Maids' with fountains, kiosks, and marble elephants.

Sajjangarh Palace

Hilltop palace offering panoramic views of the city lakes.

Fateh Sagar Lake

Popular for boating and the Nehru Park island.

Important Precautions

  • Wear life jackets during boat rides
  • Be careful of slippery steps near ghats
  • Book hotels in advance during wedding season
  • Verify antique purchases for authenticity
  • Respect privacy in residential heritage areas

Things You Must Carry

  • Light cottons for day, light woolens for winter evenings
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses
  • Camera for the stunning architecture
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Mosquito repellent

Travel Tips

Enjoy a sunset dinner at a rooftop restaurant overlooking the lake. Take a ropeway ride to Karni Mata Temple for city views. Watch a cultural show at Bagore Ki Haveli.

How to Reach Udaipur

  • By Air: Maharana Pratap Airport (~22km away).
  • By Train: Udaipur City Railway Station.
  • By Road: Well connected by NH8 from Jaipur and Ahmedabad.
