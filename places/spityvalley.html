SPITI VALLEY

A raw land of monasteries, mountains, and silence

About Spiti Valley

Spiti Valley is a cold desert mountain valley located in Himachal Pradesh. Known for its rugged terrain and ancient monasteries, Spiti is one of the most remote regions in India.

Life here is slow, peaceful, and deeply connected to Tibetan Buddhist culture.

Best Time to Visit Spiti Valley

  • June – September: Best accessibility
  • Winter: Extreme cold, limited access

Places to Visit in Spiti

Key Monastery

Largest monastery in Spiti Valley with ancient scriptures.

Chandratal Lake

High-altitude crescent-shaped lake with stunning reflections.

Kaza

Main town and cultural heart of Spiti Valley.

Dhankar Monastery

Ancient monastery perched on dramatic cliffs.

Travel Tips & Precautions

  • Acclimatization is mandatory
  • Carry extra fuel and food
  • Medical facilities are limited
  • Internet connectivity is weak
