DARJEELING

“The Queen of the Hills, where tea gardens meet the clouds.”

About Darjeeling

Darjeeling is a colonial-era hill station in West Bengal, famous for its tea industry and the UNESCO World Heritage Darjeeling Himalayan Railway. Perched at an elevation of 6,700 ft, it offers breathtaking views of Kanchenjunga, the world's third-highest peak.

With its sprawling tea estates, colonial architecture, and vibrant culture, Darjeeling is a perfect getaway for nature lovers, honeymooners, and tea connoisseurs.

Best Time to Visit Darjeeling

  • April to June: Pleasant summer, ideal for sightseeing
  • October to December: Clear skies, best mountain views
  • July to September: Monsoon, lush green but misty

Top Places to Visit in Darjeeling

Tiger Hill

Famous for the spectacular sunrise view over Kanchenjunga and Everest.

Batasia Loop

A scenic spiral railway track with a war memorial and garden.

Japanese Peace Pagoda

A symbol of peace and spirituality with panoramic valley views.

Padmaja Naidu Zoo

Home to the rare Red Panda and Snow Leopard.

Happy Valley Tea Estate

Walk through lush green tea bushes and witness tea processing.

Rock Garden

A beautiful terraced garden with waterfalls and rock sculptures.

Important Precautions

  • Roads can be steep and winding; carry motion sickness meds
  • Water scarcity can happen; use water judiciously
  • Be prepared for sudden weather changes
  • Verify toy train schedules in advance
  • Carry cash for local markets

Things You Must Carry

  • Light woolens for summer, heavy for winter
  • Umbrella or raincoat (weather is unpredictable)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Camera for the stunning landscapes
  • Valid ID proofs

Travel Tips

Don't miss a ride on the Toy Train (Joy Ride). Try the local momos and thukpa. Wake up deeply early for the Tiger Hill sunrise (around 3 AM). Respect the local culture and keep the hills clean.

How to Reach Darjeeling

  • By Air: Bagdogra Airport (IXB) is the nearest, ~70km away.
  • By Train: New Jalpaiguri (NJP) is the major railhead.
  • By Road: Taxis and buses available from Siliguri/NJP (3-4 hours).
