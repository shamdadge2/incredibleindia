MANALI

A perfect blend of nature, adventure, and culture

About Manali

Manali is one of the most popular hill stations in India, located in Himachal Pradesh. Nestled along the Beas River, it is surrounded by snow-covered peaks, pine forests, and lush valleys. Manali attracts honeymooners, families, backpackers, and adventure enthusiasts from all over the world.

From peaceful temples to thrilling adventure sports, Manali offers something for every traveler.

Best Time to Visit Manali

  • March – June: Pleasant weather, sightseeing, adventure sports
  • July – September: Monsoon, landslides possible
  • October – February: Snowfall, winter activities

Top Places to Visit in Manali

Solang Valley

Adventure hub for skiing, paragliding, zorbing, and snow activities.

Rohtang Pass

High mountain pass offering panoramic views and snow experiences.

Hadimba Temple

Ancient wooden temple surrounded by cedar forests.

Old Manali

Laid-back area known for cafés, culture, and riverside views.

Things to Do in Manali

  • River rafting in Beas River
  • Skiing and snowboarding
  • Mountain biking and trekking
  • Café hopping in Old Manali

Important Precautions

  • Check weather conditions before travel
  • Avoid night driving in mountains
  • Carry warm clothes even in summer
