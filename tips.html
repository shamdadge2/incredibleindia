Travel Tips & Precautions

Smart travel makes great memories. Travel prepared.

  • Research the destination weather and best time to visit
  • Book hotels and transport in advance
  • Check local rules, permits, or restrictions
  • Inform family or friends about your travel plan
  • Keep digital & physical copies of ID proofs

üéí Essentials You Must Carry

  • Government ID (Aadhar / Passport)
  • Cash + ATM card (not all places accept UPI)
  • Mobile phone, charger, power bank
  • Basic medicines & first-aid kit
  • Comfortable footwear and clothes

ü©∫ Health & Safety Precautions

  • Drink bottled or purified water only
  • Avoid street food if your stomach is sensitive
  • Carry personal medicines if you have medical conditions
  • Use sunscreen in hot regions and moisturizer in cold areas
  • Rest properly to avoid travel fatigue

üèî Mountain Travel Tips

  • Acclimatize slowly to avoid altitude sickness
  • Avoid alcohol at high altitude
  • Carry warm clothes even in summer
  • Follow local guides and weather warnings
  • Do not overestimate your physical limits

üèñ Beach & Coastal Travel Tips

  • Follow lifeguard instructions strictly
  • Avoid swimming during high tide
  • Protect electronics from sand & water
  • Stay hydrated and avoid sunstroke
  • Respect marine life and environment

üõï Cultural & Local Etiquette

  • Dress modestly in temples and rural areas
  • Remove footwear before entering religious places
  • Ask before clicking photos of people
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Learn a few basic local words if possible

üí∞ Money & Budget Tips

  • Do not carry large amounts of cash
  • Keep money in multiple places
  • Confirm prices before booking rides or guides
  • Avoid scams and unofficial agents
  • Track expenses daily

üå± Responsible Travel

  • Do not litter; carry waste back if needed
  • Avoid plastic as much as possible
  • Respect wildlife and natural habitats
  • Support local businesses and guides
  • Leave the place better than you found it
